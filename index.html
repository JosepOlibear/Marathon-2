About the Project

TrekTribe is a web application that aims to help cyclists and runners find different routes and build connections with one another, encouraging more engagement in their activities.

Core Features

Feature One

Share your travel experiences by posting images along with captivating descriptions!

Feature Two

Embark on adventures and earn XP as you explore, leveling up in the process!

Feature Three

Discover generated tracks nearby, tailored to your preferred difficulty level for an exciting journey!
